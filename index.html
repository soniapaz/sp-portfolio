01. About me

I'm a curious and determined person who enjoys challenges and loves learning.

I grew up in an environment where both Spanish and English were spoken, and where computers and video games were very common. It didn't take me long to become interested in both.

I studied Translation and Interpreting, which gave me communication and organizational skills and the ability to work paying great attention to detail, context, and layout.

That's when I discovered that programming existed. It really caught my attention, but life took me down a different path. My coding journey didn't start until a year ago, when I decided to attend a workshop that would change my way of seeing, understanding and interacting with the web.

The rest of the story is told through my projects and the technologies I'm currently working with:

  • HTML
  • JavaScript
  • CSS
  • React
  • Bootstrap
